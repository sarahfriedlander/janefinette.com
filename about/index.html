I am a bilingual marketing communications professional specializing in supporting technology companies, small businesses and startups.

For the last 15 years of my career I have been fortunate to build out and execute marketing plans that have utilized engagement marketing strategies, email marketing campaigns and social media for: Apple, Adobe, Mozilla Firefox, Seagate and may others.

Originally from Long Island, and a graduate of Rochester Institute of Technology I have lived in both New York, the San Francisco Bay Area, and even called Santiago de Chile home for 5 years.

Since the end of 2015, I am now back in New York and living in Manhattan and soaking up all life has to offer in the "greatest city in the world."

Today I am a Director of Marketing for W-Systems Corp., a SugarCRM Elite Partner with notable expertise in CRM deployments for financial services, professional services, technology, manufacturing, medical devices and higher education organizations.

When not working my free time is spent with my husband Rodrigo and our dog Aidan. I am a self-professed geek, I love traveling, trying new food, wine tasting, listening to music and meeting new people.
