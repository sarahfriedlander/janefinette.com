I am a bilingual marketing communications professional specializing in supporting technology companies, small businesses and startups.

For the last 10 years of my career I have been fortunate to build out and execute marketing plans that have utilized engagement marketing strategies, email marketing campaigns and social media for: Apple, Adobe, Mozilla Firefox, Seagate and may others.

Originally from Long Island, and a graduate of Rochester Institute of Technology I have earned my bicoastal badge living in both New York and the San Francisco Bay area, but now am proud to call Santiago de Chile home.

Santiago provides the perfect blend of many of my passions: a wonderful language, city living and an increasing exciting technology scene.

Today I am a bilingual marketing consultant, working for a variety of international small businesses and startups located in Australia, Chile and the United States.

When not working my free time is spent with my husband Rodrigo and our dog Aidan. I am a self-professed geek, I love traveling, trying new food, wine tasting, listening to music and meeting new people.
