ABOUT | YOU FOUND ME

I’ve lived an eccletic, rich and full life – and I’m passionate about people living their’s.

From pioneering ecommerce in the UK, to democratizing art, to empowering the largest open source community in the world; I’ve done some pretty impressive things by those interested in measuring career success. Little known facts, I also worked on a factory floor for three years making hats, had two failed start-ups, and spent two years racing cars.

And through the high points, and what appear as not so high times, a natural thread ran through my experiences – a dedication to helping others: find their path, create happiness and live their impact.

Two years ago I finally saw this for myself, and became a coach. Every day I set out to create meaningful change by empowering people to live their greatness.

VITA | ALL ABOUT ME

For the first 20 years of my career I built and empowered global teams and communities for: eBay, Mozilla Firefox, Jungle.com, Astia, Gooru and many others, reaching hundreds of millions of users globally through all stages of growth. I founded two consulting practices helping entrepreneurs and communities expand globally, and brought two US based software companies to EMEA.

In 2012 I transformed my career to leadership development, which is my passion. My career has been rich and full, and without exception – building deep rooted connection and meaning has always been my mission.

WORK | PRESENT AND FUTURE

Today, I head up stride»today, a leadership and executive coaching firm based in Silicon Valley, helping individuals and organizations unleash their potential. I deliver comprehensive leadership initiatives, coach executives 1:1, and lead in-house ‘office-hour’ coaching programs. I am a trained Co-Active Coach.

Clients comprise a range of organizations including Mozilla, Indiegogo, Facebook and startup founders. I bring real world experience as an executive, strategic advisor, and board member of numerous global organizations.

Furthermore, I am the owner of Equanimity Coaching - offering unique coaching and leadership programs working with the horse as our guide. Horses provide a profoundly pure feedback opportunity that enables us to deeply work on non-verbal communication, purpose and leadership.

I am also the founder of The Coaching Fellowship, an international community of coaches dedicated to helping young professional women reach their full potential, and a non-profit (Mentor for Good).
