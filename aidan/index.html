This is the page dedicated for Aidan, my dog. In the words of Aidan:

My name is Aidan and I am a Cavachon (a cross between the Cavalier King Charles Spaniel and Bichon Frise). I was born on May 17, 2008 in Grundy Center, Iowa. On August 15, 2008 my entire life changed and I was put in a crate and transported to the airport. Two plane rides later, I landed at San Francisco Airport. After a little over two years in California, I moved with my mom down to Santiago, Chile and am happy to be a little Chileno. I’m spoiled with lots of treats, love and attention. The human also thinks I am trained because I do easy things like sit and get free food. But wouldn’t you do it too?
