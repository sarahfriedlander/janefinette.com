In working together you will be guided in thought-provoking and creative approaches. You will garner fresh perspectives, achieve clarity and increase awareness. My role is to encourage, guide, and motivate you through the challenges and opportunities you are facing; and toward the change you want to see.

CLIENTS

My clients range from startup CEOs, professionals at companies such as Google and Mozilla, and many more who have chosen a path toward self-awareness and fulfillment.

WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT

A good coach is someone who is an expert at helping others create positive change in their lives. I will help you explore and come up with the best choices for you based on where you are and your future vision. I do not "fix" or give advice, I focus on your strengths, and changing behaviors to achieve desired outcomes. The coaching relationship is unilateral – it is exclusively focused on you and your goals.

Coaching sessions are two to four sessions per month, lasting at least 30 minutes and up to 60 minutes.

One-on-one meetings with clients in an office is possible, but I recommend telephone sessions for the ease of use, minimization of distractions, better privacy, and efficiency.

RATES

Individual coaching packages consist of a two hour long discovery session and two hours of monthly coaching. Assignments begin at $250 per month for a minimum length of three months.
